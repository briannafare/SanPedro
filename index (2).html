Experiences & Adventures

Iguana Eco Sanctuary

Mangrove conservation area

6

On the lagoon side of town (Seaweed Street, near the power substation). A peaceful spot to see iguanas up close in their natural habitat.

Go around 10 a.m. when they feed the iguanas ‚Äî that's when they all come out and it's amazing to see. Donation-based, open ~8am-4:30pm.

Fun Night Out

Chicken Drop

Corona Del Mar ‚Äì Thursday nights

10

The "world-famous" Chicken Drop (chicken bingo) at Corona Del Mar Hotel, south of town. Every Thursday evening starting around 6pm.

You buy a number on a big board and everyone waits to see where the chicken "drops." It's ridiculous, hilarious, and you have to experience it at least once.
